Analyze the following sentence/phrase found in an Anki card:
"{Expression}"

Provide a concise but helpful explanation suitable for the "Notes" field.

1. **Do NOT provide a full English translation of the sentence.**
2. Explain the meaning of the key vocabulary or phrases in English.
3. If there is a Cloze deletion ({{c1::...}}), focus on explaining the meaning and usage of the hidden part (the token between double ::).
4. Point out any interesting grammar, usage, or nuances relevant to the language of the expression.
5. Keep it brief (under 40 words) and clear.
6. Output ONLY the note content, no "Here is the note:" prefix.
7. Use standard Markdown formatting (bold key terms, etc.).

## Example

Input: `{{c1::Kal::Tomorrow}} phir se ek naya din hoga.`
Output: `Kal means "tomorrow" or "yesterday." The future verb hoga (will be) confirms it means "tomorrow." Phir se means
"again" and naya is "new."`
